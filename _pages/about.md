# Research Interest
I am currently interested in Representation Learning, Multilingual Models, Low-Resource NLP and Evaluation Methods. 

My focus lies in exploring the impact of word representation systems within modern language model architectures, particularly in the context of languages with diverse linguistic properties or limited data availability.

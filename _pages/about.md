I am a NLP student reseacher at Ben Gurion Universiy, advised by [Yuval Pinter](https://www.yuvalpinter.com/), and a Software Engineer at Meta. My full [CV is available here.](/files/cv.pdf)

# Research Interest
I am broadly interested in understanding Language Models, their potential capabilities,
inherent limitations, and future social implications. I’m currently focused on exploring the
limitations imposed on LLMs by their foundational word representation schemes,
particularly in the context of languages with diverse linguistic properties or limited data
availability.

Research

I am broadly interested in understanding language models as modular systems, developing effective system-level evaluations, and designing methods for smoother end-to-end optimization.

My current research focuses on:
1. Evaluating the constraints imposed on LLMs by word representation schemes (Tokenization) and advancing token-free solutions.
2. Investigating the limitations of modern retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) architectures and enhancing retriever-LM interactions.

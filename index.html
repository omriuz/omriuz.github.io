Omri Uzan

I am an incoming Computer Science Ph.D. student at Stanford University, where I will be a member of the Natural Language Processing Group and Stanford AI Lab, beginning in September 2025.

I currently work as a software engineer at Meta, where I focus on applying retrieval models for search engine optimization (SEO) across Meta's products.

I completed my B.Sc. and M.Sc. in Computer Science at Ben Gurion University, where I conducted NLP research under the guidance of Yuval Pinter.

During my studies, I worked for two and a half years as an instructor at Nitzanim, a non-profit organization dedicated to narrowing social gaps through STEM education.
